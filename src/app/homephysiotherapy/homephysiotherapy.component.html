Physiotherapy & Rehab at Home

As we age, our bodies undergo a natural process of gradual strength and mobility decline, which can lead to various health concerns, depending on our lifestyles. However, with the right expertise and a well-structured physiotherapy plan, you can take proactive steps to ensure that you or your loved ones receive the most tailored and effective care. When you partner with Parvathy Home Care Services, our highly trained and experienced physiotherapists conduct a comprehensive initial assessment in the comfort of your own home. Based on this assessment, they provide expert guidance and initiate therapy as per the patient's unique needs.

Physiotherapy has a proven track record in effectively restoring and enhancing physical strength and mobility. Parvathy Home Care Services makes it convenient for you to receive these essential services within your home environment. Our experienced physiotherapists utilize advanced techniques and portable equipment to offer various forms of therapy, addressing issues such as pain relief, improved strength, posture, motor control, enhanced endurance, gait improvement, and sensory enhancement.

Our team of practitioners at Parvathy holds specialized degrees in various fields, including Neurological, Cardio-respiratory, and Musculoskeletal issues. Years of clinical experience equip them to competently address a wide range of challenges faced by you or your loved ones.
