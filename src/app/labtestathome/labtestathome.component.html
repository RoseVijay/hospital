Doctor's Visit

In today's fast-paced world, it's increasingly crucial to prioritize your health. Regular check-ups are essential for individuals of all ages. Unexpectedly, you may find yourself in need of a doctor's expertise for conditions like fevers, illnesses, or chronic ailments. Delaying a doctor's visit can heighten the risks associated with a health issue and may lead to complications necessitating a hospital stay. The best and easiest way to mitigate this is to act promptly when symptoms arise. If leaving the comfort of your familiar surroundings to visit a clinic or hospital presents challenges, consider Parvathy Home Care Services, where doctors come to you.

Research has demonstrated that patients recover faster in a familiar environment, as opposed to a hospital setting. Parvathy Home Care Services offers physician services for individuals dealing with health issues who struggle to access a doctor's office or are bedridden. This entails the visitation of an experienced doctor to the patient's home, either on a regular basis or in emergencies.

During the first visit, the physician assesses the patient's condition, reviews their treatment plan, examines lab reports, medications, past care, and monitors their progress. Subsequently, based on the patient's condition, the doctor provides immediate care, arranges consultations with qualified specialists, or recommends hospitalization if necessary.

Parvathy Home Care Services' general physicians are available for emergency visits and consultations around the clock, providing expert guidance that can alleviate patient discomfort and reduce family anxiety. Regular physician visits ensure timely check-ups and comprehensive monitoring, ensuring the patient's condition remains stable or their recovery is on track.

Lab Test at Home

At Parvathy Home Care, we recognize the importance of accurate and timely diagnostic lab tests in healthcare. Our comprehensive laboratory testing services are designed to provide you with precise results in a convenient and comfortable setting.

Whether you require routine checkups, specialized tests, or post-treatment monitoring, our state-of-the-art laboratory facilities and experienced professionals are here to meet your diagnostic needs.

Our Lab Testing Services

  • Blood Tests: Comprehensive blood screenings for various health parameters, including cholesterol levels, glucose, and more.
  • Pathology Tests: Evaluation of tissue, fluid, and cell samples to diagnose diseases and conditions.
  • Urinalysis: Examination of urine samples to assess kidney function, detect infections, and monitor overall health.
  • Imaging Services: Radiology and imaging services, including X-rays, MRIs, and CT scans.
  • Specialized Tests: Targeted tests for specific health concerns, such as cancer markers, hormonal imbalances, and genetic testing.
  • Post-Treatment Monitoring: Follow-up tests to track your progress after medical treatments and surgeries.
  • Wellness Panels: Customized health checkup panels to assess your overall well-being.
